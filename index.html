About

The terminal is where digital freedom is fought

Most content about digital freedom looks the same: talking head on camera, stock footage, AI-generated voiceover explaining why privacy matters. It's become background noise — easy to scroll past, easy to forget.

This project strips all of that away. What remains is the raw narrative. Each sequence is a self-contained HTML file that simulates a terminal session, revealing data, quotes, and documented arguments about the state of personal freedoms in the digital age.

All facts sourced from public records, speeches, and documented events. Nothing is fabricated. The format itself communicates the theme — the terminal is the canvas and the message.

"In encryption, in open source, in self-hosted infrastructure, in the choice of what tools we trust with our data."
